Privacy Policy - Ready App LLC

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Ready App LLC ("Ready App," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Ready App platform, a verified trucking marketplace. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including name, email address, phone number, payment information, and trucking credentials. We also collect usage data and device information automatically.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process transactions, communicate with you, verify credentials, comply with legal obligations, and for accounting and financial management purposes including QuickBooks Online integration.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information with service providers who assist in our operations, including payment processors and accounting software providers, under confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Financial data is handled through encrypted connections and secure third-party providers.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us at peter@ready-app.com.

QuickBooks Integration

Ready App uses QuickBooks Online APIs to manage accounting and financial data. This integration is subject to Intuit QuickBooks terms of service.
