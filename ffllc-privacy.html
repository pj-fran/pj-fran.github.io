Privacy Policy - Francis Family LLC

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Francis Family LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a holding company managing multiple business entities. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information in connection with our business operations and financial management tools.

Information We Collect

We collect business and financial information necessary for accounting and operations management, including business names, contact information, financial transaction data, and tax identification numbers.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for internal accounting, financial reporting, business operations management, and compliance with legal and tax obligations. This includes integration with QuickBooks Online for financial management across our portfolio of entities.

Information Sharing

We do not sell information to third parties. We share information only with service providers necessary for business operations, including accounting software providers, under appropriate confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect business and financial information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

QuickBooks Integration

Francis Family LLC uses QuickBooks Online APIs for financial management across its business entities. This integration is subject to Intuit QuickBooks terms of service.
